Chain of Command

The Avalon Police Department operates under a structured chain of command that ensures efficient communication, clear leadership, and effective department operations. Each rank carries specific responsibilities and authority, working together to serve and protect our community.

Chief of Police

Department head responsible for overall leadership, policy-making, and strategic direction of the APD.

Deputy Chief

Second-in-command, assists the Chief in department management and can assume command when needed.

Assistant Chief

Oversees major divisions and special operations, reporting directly to the Deputy Chief.

Major

Commands operational units and implements departmental policies and strategies.

Captain

Manages precinct operations and supervises multiple units within their command.

Lieutenant

Oversees shift operations and serves as a bridge between command staff and patrol officers.

Sergeant

First-line supervisors who directly oversee patrol officers and daily operations.

Senior Officer

Experienced officers who mentor newer officers and handle specialized assignments.

Officer

Patrol officers who serve as the primary point of contact with the community.
